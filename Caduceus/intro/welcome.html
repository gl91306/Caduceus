What is Remedical?

Remedical is a website used to help people with alzheimers keep track of daily activities to help make their lives easier! From brushing your teeth to taking your medication, Remedical will remind you all of it. Not only does our website notify set reminders, but it also sends self-care tips to make sure our users are making sure to take care of themselves!
